Refund Policy

At visamate.co.uk, we aim to provide transparent and reliable visa services. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a payment.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Duplicate Payment: If you have been charged twice for the same service.
  • Service Not Delivered: If we are unable to process your application due to our error.
  • Cancelled Application: If you cancel your application before we begin processing it.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • If your visa is rejected by the embassy/consulate.
  • If your application has already been submitted or is under processing.
  • If incorrect or incomplete information is provided by you.

Refund Process

Refund requests must be made in writing via email to info@visamate.co.uk within 7 days of the payment.

Once approved, refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days.

Refunds will be made to the original payment method used.
